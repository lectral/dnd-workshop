# Tarot Reading Guide

## Built-in spread positions

### 3-card spread
1. Past Shadow
2. Present Pressure
3. Future Unfolding

### 5-card spread
1. Signifier
2. Tension
3. Hidden Force
4. Crossroads
5. Outcome

## Interpretation flow

1. Deal the spread with `node scripts/deal-spread.js --spread 3 --format json` or `--spread 5`.
2. Read each card's meaning and Adventure Seed. Treat the seed text, and the script field named `prompt`, as an illustrative example rather than a required result.
3. Translate symbols into campaign-facing material: threats, allies, omens, locations, emotional truths, or rewards.
4. Write `## 🔮 The Reading` as a coherent interpretation.
5. Write `## ✨ The Divination` with five concrete ideas.

## Good uses

- campaign chapter omens
- villain motives
- quest complications
- NPC destiny beats
- recurring symbolism
- session openers
- scene reframing when stuck
